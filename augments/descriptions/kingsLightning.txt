A global ability, usable once per game. Press its slot on your global ability row (A/S/D/F) to call down a permanent aura of lightning around your King — every square touching it, in every direction. Casting I Am Lightning uses up your turn — you do not also get to make a normal move. Once active it never turns off: from then on, any unit standing on one of those squares at the end of either player's turn is executed, friend or foe, and it keeps re-checking every square your King moves next to for the rest of the game.
