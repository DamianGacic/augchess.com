Exchanges both your rooks for Trolls. A Troll still walks along ranks and files like a rook, but only one square at a time — and it can only step onto open ground, never capturing by moving into a unit. Instead, once every one of your turns, a Troll can club: select it and press Q, then choose one of the quadrants next to its own square — orthogonally, not diagonally — to flatten every unit standing there, friend or foe. Clubbing must be cast before anything else that turn and uses up the whole turn, same as a normal move would.
