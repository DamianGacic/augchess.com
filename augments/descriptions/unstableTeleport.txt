Requires Apprentice Corps. Select an Apprentice and press W (or Q, if it doesn't also have Unstable Fireball) before moving anything else this turn, then choose any free square on the board except the 2 rows closest to the opponent's end to teleport it there. Casting uses up your turn — you do not also get to make a normal move. The magic is volatile: 9 times in 12 it lands exactly where chosen; 1 time in 12 it drifts to a random free square next to the chosen one (or stays put if none are free); 1 time in 12 it scatters to a random free square anywhere on the board (still never the opponent's very last row); 1 time in 12 the Apprentice is lost entirely.
