A global ability, usable once per game. Press its slot on your global ability row (A/S/D/F) to wreathe your King in a permanent ring of fire — every square touching it, in every direction. Casting Ring of Fire uses up your turn — you do not also get to make a normal move. Once active it never turns off: any unit that stands in the ring through the end of one turn and is still standing there at the end of the very next turn — friend or foe — burns and is executed. Step out before that second turn ends and the count resets; a fresh unit arriving on that square starts its own count from zero.
