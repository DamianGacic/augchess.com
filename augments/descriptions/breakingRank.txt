Requires Ghoul Corps. A passive ability — no casting, just an ever-ready slot on your global row. At the start of each of your turns, every enemy unit sitting on a square one of your Ghouls could currently charge has a 1-in-6 chance of being struck down automatically, with that Ghoul charging onto the square just like a normal capture. This doesn't use up your turn, but a Ghoul that broke rank this way can't also be moved again until your next turn, and a Ghoul is exempt for the one turn right after it moves. With Deviant, this also covers diagonal charge squares.
