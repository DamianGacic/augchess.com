Requires Apprentice Corps. Select an Apprentice and press Q before moving anything else this turn, then choose the enemy unit sitting exactly 2 squares directly ahead of it (only if the square in between is empty) to hurl an unstable fireball at it. Casting uses up your turn — you do not also get to make a normal move. The magic is volatile: 4 times in 6 it works as intended and the target is destroyed; 1 time in 6 it backfires and both the target and the Apprentice are destroyed; 1 time in 6 it fizzles and only the Apprentice is destroyed.
